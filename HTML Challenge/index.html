Encode

[en - kohd]

verb (used with object), encoded, encoding.

1. to convert (a message, information ,etc) into code

Origin of encode circa 1930-1935

First recorded in 1930-35; en + code

Related forms

  • encodable, adjective
  • encodement, noun
  • encoder, noun
  • miscode, verb (used with object), misencoded, miscoding
