Ingredients

  • 10 to 12 medium-sized jalapeno peppers
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/3 cup Hellmann’s® Mayonnaise
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground chipotle (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 8 slices of bread Sour Dough is recommended
  • 12 slices cooked bacon (break in half)
  • 8 slices of Munster cheese (you can use your favorite cheese slices if you like)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter (softened)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees
  2. Place about 10 to 12 medium-sized jalapeno peppers on a baking sheet that has been lined with parchment paper
  3. Roast the peppers until the skins have blackened, and the peppers have turned from a bright shiny green to a dull green collar
  4. Cooking the peppers will take about 10 - 12 minutes
  5. With gloved hands, pull off the skin of the peppers
  6. Pull the stem off of the peppers, and then open the peppers up with one cut down the pepper, open the pepper and then scrape out the seeds
  7. If you like a lot of heat leave the peppers whole, if not, consider cutting them into small strips
  8. Turn the oven down to 400 degrees, you will cook the sandwiches in there
  9. In a small bowl combine cream cheese, Hellmann’s® Mayonnaise, shredded cheddar cheese, ground chipotle (optional), and Worcestershire sauce
  10. Stir until the cheese filling is uniform
  11. Prepare the sandwiches by spreading 1/4 of the cheese mixture on 4 slices of bread
  12. Place jalapeno pepper pieces on top of the cheese, you will want to cover up the cheese filling
  13. Place 6 pieces of bacon on the peppers (that's 3 pieces of whole bacon that you broke in half), and then top with 2 slices of cheese
  14. Top sandwich with the remaining slice of bread
  15. Lightly butter the outer pieces of bread on each sandwich
  16. Place sandwiches on a baking sheet
  17. Bake sandwiches for about 5 minutes or until golden brown, flip sandwiches over, and continue to bake until they are golden brown
  18. They will take less time on the outside. If you have a convection oven, be extra careful with the time
